About Cornerstone

Built on honest work.

An Israeli-founded, Hobart-based SEO team focused on steady, compounding growth — not shortcuts, not promises we can't keep. Seven core principles, one relentless commitment.

Who's behind this

Cornerstone was founded by an Israeli-born developer now based in Hobart, Tasmania. After years building and growing websites across Israel, Europe, and Australia, one pattern became impossible to ignore: most small business sites are almost completely invisible in search — not because what they offer isn't valuable, but because Google never got a clear picture of what they do.

Our team built this service around a simple belief: consistent, methodical monthly SEO work compounds into explosive, lasting growth — and every business deserves access to it without agency overhead or locked-in contracts.

How we work

Every client gets genuine, individual attention. We review your site, understand your audience and market, and make technical improvements that help Google understand exactly what you offer — and show it to the right people at the right moment.

You get a plain-English update each month. No dashboard full of metrics you don't understand — just a clear summary of what was done, what moved, and what we're tackling next. Our 13-point monthly tracking process covers rankings, technical health, and on-page signals, so nothing slips through the cracks.
