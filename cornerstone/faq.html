About SEO
What actually is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is the process of improving your website so that it appears more often — and higher — in Google search results.

When someone searches for a product, service, or topic related to your business, Google decides which websites to show. SEO is the work that makes your site one of them.

How long does it take to see results?

Honestly — it depends. Most websites start seeing meaningful movement after 3–6 months of consistent work. Some see early changes sooner, particularly after the first 7-point technical audit (our Starter plan baseline) clears major blockers.

SEO is not instant. It builds over time, and the sites that stay the course see the best — often explosive — long-term outcomes.

Can you guarantee I'll rank #1 on Google?

No — and anyone who does is not being straight with you. Google's algorithm has hundreds of factors and changes constantly. What we can guarantee is consistent, honest monthly work that measurably improves your site's visibility over time.

Is SEO better than Google Ads?

They serve different purposes. Google Ads put you in front of people immediately — but stop the moment you stop paying. SEO builds over time and keeps working without ongoing ad spend.

We offer both. Monthly SEO plans for long-term compounding growth, and a one-time Google Ads campaign if you want traffic while your SEO builds momentum.

About Our Service
What do you actually do each month?

It depends on the plan. At minimum (Starter), we run a 7-point technical audit — title tags, meta descriptions, H1 structure, canonical tags, XML sitemap, robots.txt, schema markup — and fix everything we find.

Higher plans (Growth, Pro, Authority) add ongoing on-page optimisation, keyword expansion, backlink audits, competitor gap analysis, page speed work, schema implementation, and more. Every month you receive a plain-English summary of what was done and what changed.

Do you write content for my site?

Our plans focus on technical and structural SEO. Pro and Authority plans include a monthly content brief — a fully researched plan for a new page — but actual content writing is up to you or your team to implement.

How many websites do you work on?

Cornerstone is a solo operation. We deliberately keep the client list small so every site gets genuine attention — not a template approach. If we're at capacity, we'll let you know upfront.

What do you need from me to get started?

Just your website URL. After sign-up we'll review your site and reach out if we need anything else — like Google Search Console access for higher-tier plans. You don't need to prepare anything in advance.

What's the difference between the SEO plans and a Google Ads campaign?

The one-time Google Ads campaign is a single payment that runs paid ads immediately. It's a short-term boost — ideal to run while your SEO is building in the background.
